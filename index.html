What Driftline Does

Learning Strategy
Strong courses begin with clear direction. Before development starts, the structure of the learning needs to be defined — outcomes, progression, delivery model, and assessment approach. Driftline works with organisations to translate expertise into a practical learning framework that guides development and supports confident delivery.
Course & Subject Design
Design turns expertise into a coherent learning experience. Courses need clear structure, purposeful learning activities, and assessment that supports genuine understanding. Driftline designs subjects and courses that are logically structured, easy to navigate, and aligned to the outcomes they are intended to achieve.
Course Review & Redesign
Courses naturally evolve over time. Content grows, assessment shifts, and structure can become less visible to learners. Driftline reviews existing courses and programs to clarify structure, strengthen alignment, and redesign elements that no longer support effective learning.
Digital Learning Development
Digital courses require more than simply placing materials online. Structure, navigation, and interaction need to work clearly within the platform where the learning takes place. Driftline builds structured online learning experiences inside your LMS, ensuring courses function clearly and reliably for both learners and staff.

About Driftline

Driftline was created to bridge the gap between expertise and execution.

Many organisations have deep knowledge and capable people — but turning that knowledge into a structured learning experience requires careful planning and design judgement.

Driftline works closely with clients to shape ideas into clear, purposeful learning. Projects are intentionally selective, allowing space for careful thinking, strong design decisions, and attention to detail.
